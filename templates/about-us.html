About us

Al Rawahi.

Al Rawahi Projects is a business-focused company specializing in representing innovative businesses and advanced technologies that add real value across multiple industries. We work closely with partners whose products and solutions are driven by quality, innovation, and market relevance. Our role is to connect these businesses with the right markets, customers, and commercial opportunities through strategic representation and professional market support.

Al Rawahi Projects operates through six main divisions, covering company representation, import and export, tires, batteries and lubricants, forklift solutions, swimming pool solutions, and global sourcing. Each division is designed to support our partners with tailored solutions that meet market demands and commercial objectives. As a trusted representative, we support our partners by managing sales activities, conducting competition and market analysis, representing brands directly in front of customers, identifying the right market channels, implementing effective marketing strategies, and supporting commercial negotiations. Our commitment is to build long-term partnerships based on trust, performance, and sustainable business growth.
